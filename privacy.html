Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 15, 2025

Welcome to Sharky Chat. Your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our chat application.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services and improve your experience. Types of information we collect include:

  • Personal Information: Username, email address, profile picture, and any information you provide during registration or profile updates.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interactions with Sharky Chat such as chat logs, device information, IP address, and activity timestamps.
  • Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies to maintain your session and enhance user experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate and manage your account securely.
  • To enable real-time messaging and chatroom functionalities.
  • To enhance and personalize your user experience.
  • To communicate important updates, including changes to our services or policies.
  • To ensure compliance with legal obligations and to prevent fraudulent activities.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent or at your direction.
  • With trusted third-party service providers who help us operate and improve Sharky Chat (under strict confidentiality agreements).
  • When required by law or to respond to lawful requests by public authorities.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage method is 100% secure.

Your Choices and Rights

  • You may update or correct your profile information at any time.
  • You can control your notification settings within the app.
  • You can delete your account, which permanently removes your personal data from our systems.

Children’s Privacy

Sharky Chat is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting a notice in the app or via email. Your continued use of Sharky Chat after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us
