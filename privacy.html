Privacy Policy

EFFECTIVE DATE: AUGUST 15, 2025

Welcome to Sharky Chat. We recognize that privacy is a fundamental right. This Comprehensive Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to transparency regarding the collection, utilization, and protection of your personal metadata and communication data within our ecosystem.

1. Information Acquisition

In order to maintain a secure and optimized messaging environment, we process the following categories of information:

  • Identity Metadata: Includes your unique username, verified email address, and profile assets provided during account registration.
  • Telemetric & Technical Data: We automatically process IP addresses, precise geographic locations, hardware specifications (OS, browser type), and network service provider details for security auditing.
  • Behavioral Logs: Interaction timestamps, session durations, and real-time presence indicators to facilitate seamless communication.

2. Operational Data Utilization

The acquired data is strictly utilized for high-priority operational functions:

  • Authentication: Ensuring secure access and safeguarding against unauthorized account takeovers.
  • Service Optimization: Utilizing technical metadata to adapt the application interface to your specific device architecture.
  • Security Integrity: Proactively monitoring for fraudulent activities, spam patterns, and policy violations to maintain a safe community.

3. Data Confidentiality

Sharky Chat maintains a strict Non-Commercial Data Policy. We do not engage in the sale, rental, or trade of user information. Data sharing is exclusively limited to:

  • Authorized Service Infrastructure: Trusted cloud and analytics partners who comply with international data protection standards.
  • Legal Mandates: Disclosure only when legally compelled by valid subpoenas or governmental regulatory frameworks.

4. Cryptographic Security

We employ Advanced Encryption Standards (AES) and secure access controls to fortify your data against unauthorized breaches. While we utilize industry-leading protocols, users acknowledge that no digital transmission is entirely immune to sophisticated external threats.

5. User Autonomy

You retain full sovereignty over your data, including the right to Request Access, Rectification, or Permanent Erasure of your account. Deletion requests result in the irreversible removal of your records from our primary production databases.
